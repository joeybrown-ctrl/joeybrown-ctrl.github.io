About Me

Uniquely positioned due to excellent interpersonal skills learned through customer service, co-managing as part of a bar management team, training new employees, scheduling, events/social media management, mediation and conflict resolution. Demonstrated technical skills include HTML 5, CSS 3, Bootstrap 4, Git, the command-line, and GitHub thus far. Education background as a certified counselor in the modality of Depth Hypnosis through the Foundation of the Sacred Stream in Berkeley, CA; and currently working towards a certificate in full-stack web development through the University of Arizona/Trilogy in Tucson, AZ.

I originally hail from Wellington, New Zealand, and I've also lived, loved and worked in London and New York. I consider myself to be a well-seasoned traveler and international person. I am passionate about understanding both people and systems. In my spare time, I can be found cooking delicious meals, making herbal potions with my small apothecary, and tending to the garden I maintain with my tight-knit community of neighbors and friends.
