About Me

Hi! I'm Joey Brown, a full-stack web/software engineer in the making. I enjoy the continuous process of learning that web-development requires, and I approach new information with a hungry curiosity.


Before my entrance into the tech field, I worked as a bartender specializing in craft cocktails in Seattle, San Francisco and Tucson. I originally hail from Wellington, New Zealand, and I've also lived, loved and worked in London and New York. I consider myself to be a well-seasoned traveler and international person. I am passionate about understanding both people and systems. In my spare time, I can be found cooking delicious meals, making herbal potions with my small apothecary, and tending to the garden I maintain with my tight-knit community of neighbors and friends.
