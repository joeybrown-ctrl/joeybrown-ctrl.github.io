About Me

Howdy! I'm Junior Full-Stack engineer. My customer-facing background in the Food and Bev industry uniquely positions me to succeed in this role due to excellent interpersonal and problem-solving skills learned through customer service, co-managing as part of a bar management team, training new employees, scheduling, events/social media management, and mediation and conflict resolution. Current technical skills include HTML 5, CSS 3, Bootstrap 4, Git, the command-line, GitHub, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, Web APIs, and Node.js thus far. My education background includes certification as a counselor through the Foundation of the Sacred Stream in Berkeley, CA; and I'm currently working towards a certificate in full-stack web development through the University of Arizona/Trilogy in Tucson, AZ.

Before my entrance into the tech field, I worked as a bartender specializing in craft cocktails in Seattle, San Francisco and Tucson. I originally hail from Wellington, New Zealand, and I've also lived and worked in London and New York. I consider myself to be a well-seasoned traveler and international man of mystery. I am passionate about understanding both people and systems. In my spare time, I can be found cooking delicious meals, making herbal potions with my small apothecary, and tending to the garden I maintain with my tight-knit community of neighbors and friends.

I will be relocating to Auckland, New Zealand in late February 2021, and am available for remote work at this time.
